MTE211 · Instrumentation & Measurements · May 2026

Water Turbidity Measurement System

Embedded Systems Engineer

Real-time water quality monitor built on an Arduino UNO and an analog turbidity sensor. Raw ADC values are mapped to a 0–100 turbidity scale and checked against a threshold of 25 — below is reported CLEAR (green LED), above is DIRTY (red LED) — with live numeric and status output on a 16×2 I2C LCD refreshed every 100ms. Tested across clean tap water (~680 ADC, CLEAR) through highly turbid samples (~150 ADC, ~80% turbidity, DIRTY).
